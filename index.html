Spatial UX for cooking in augmented reality

Creative ideation, VR wireframing, testing, and product management for an AR cooking utility developed on the Qualcomm ThinkReality A24 head-mounted device. This project provided critical insight into UI/UX design for spatial and hands-free environments.
